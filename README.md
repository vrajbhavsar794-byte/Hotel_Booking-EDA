# 🏨 Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Hotel Booking dataset to uncover patterns influencing booking cancellations and customer behavior. The analysis focuses on identifying trends across hotel types, booking channels, lead time, pricing, and customer segments to derive actionable business insights.

The project demonstrates the complete EDA workflow, including data cleaning, visualization, correlation analysis, and business-oriented recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze hotel booking patterns and cancellation trends.
* Identify factors contributing to booking cancellations.
* Compare booking behavior across different hotel types.
* Explore customer demographics and booking channels.
* Generate actionable business insights through data visualization.

---

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* City Hotels experienced a higher number of booking cancellations compared to Resort Hotels.
* Longer lead times were associated with an increased likelihood of cancellations.
* Certain market segments showed significantly higher cancellation rates than others.
* Deposit type had a noticeable impact on customer cancellation behavior.
* Average Daily Rate (ADR) varied across booking categories and influenced cancellation patterns.
* Seasonal trends affected booking demand throughout the year.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

* Encourage non-refundable or partial deposit booking policies to reduce cancellations.
* Develop targeted retention strategies for high-cancellation customer segments.
* Implement dynamic pricing based on seasonal demand.
* Optimize marketing efforts toward customer segments with lower cancellation rates.
* Improve booking confirmation and reminder systems for customers with longer lead times.
