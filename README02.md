# 📦 Supply Chain Performance Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

Supply chain management plays a crucial role in ensuring operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and business profitability. This project performs an in-depth **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a supply chain dataset to uncover patterns related to products, suppliers, inventory, manufacturing, logistics, and revenue.

The objective is to identify operational inefficiencies, analyze business performance, and provide actionable recommendations that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company wants to improve its supply chain by answering questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which suppliers perform the best?
- How do shipping costs vary across transportation modes?
- Are inventory levels aligned with product demand?
- Which operational factors influence overall business performance?

## 📋 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Understanding
- Loaded the dataset
- Explored dataset dimensions
- Reviewed column names
- Checked data types
- Generated descriptive statistics
- Identified missing values and duplicate records

### 2️⃣ Data Cleaning
- Created a copy of the dataset
- Verified missing values
- Removed duplicate records
- Checked data types
- Explored categorical variables
- Reviewed numerical summaries

### 3️⃣ Univariate Analysis
- Distribution of numerical variables
- Box plots for outlier detection
- Frequency distribution of categorical variables
- Descriptive statistical analysis

### 4️⃣ Bivariate Analysis
- Product Type vs Revenue Generated
- Transportation Mode vs Shipping Cost
- Price vs Revenue Generated
- Supplier vs Manufacturing Cost
- Stock Levels vs Products Sold
- Product Type vs Defect Rate

### 5️⃣ Multivariate Analysis
- Revenue by Product Type and Transportation Mode
- Manufacturing Cost by Supplier and Product Type
- Pair Plot of numerical variables
- Correlation Heatmap
- Revenue by Inspection Results and Transportation Mode
- Stock Levels by Product Type and Supplier

### 6️⃣ Business Insights & Recommendations
- Identified operational trends
- Evaluated supplier performance

- Generated logistics recommendations
